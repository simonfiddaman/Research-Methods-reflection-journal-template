## Summary

In Week 7, we have taken time to reflect upon the research process and the role the researcher plays within that process. We have broadened our understanding of evaluation of good quality research and considered reflexivity as a means by which to monitor researcher bias and subjectivities in the research process. We finish by reflecting on the learning from the module, reviewing what research skills we have and what we still need to be able to confidently embark on our independent research projects.
